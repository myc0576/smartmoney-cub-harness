# Philosophy

The project is built around review, restraint, and evidence.

## Cycle, Timing, Position, Change, Restraint

The 易经 vocabulary is useful here as a review language:

- **cycle**: what phase did the trader think the market was in?
- **timing**: was the observation available before the decision?
- **position**: where was the trade idea in its lifecycle?
- **change**: what evidence would invalidate the idea?
- **restraint**: what should be left untouched?

This is not prediction by symbols or mystic forecasting. It is a way to make subjective judgment inspectable.

## Systems Engineering

The harness follows a systems-engineering posture:

- decompose the workflow;
- log inputs and outputs;
- review delayed outcomes;
- update rules only through explicit gates;
- keep humans responsible for final judgment.
